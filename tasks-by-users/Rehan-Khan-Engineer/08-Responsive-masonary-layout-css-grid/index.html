1. Wash hands with soap and water after touching animals and animal products.

2. When coughing and sneezing cover mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue.

3. Avoid travel if you have a fever and cough.

4. If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing seek medical care early.

5. Eat only well cooked food.

6. Avoid close contacts with the animals that are sick.

7. If you are coughing and sneezing, wear a mask and must know how to use it and dispose of it properly.
